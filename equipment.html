PIPELINES

Rubber compounds are manufactured in-house using high-quality raw materials with a highly controlled manufacturing process. Pipelines produced by our company have a lifetime up to 5 times longer and weigh 2-2.5 times less compared to steel pipelines.

Using our product pipelines is much more advantageous compared to steel ones since economic and process benefits may be achieved, such as:

  • * Buoyancy, flexibility, lack of ball connections (ensures reduced hydraulic losses up to 20%).
  • * Easy to mount and dismantle, enabling usage in small rivers and offshore areas.
  • * Applied as part of a floating dredge ship, reducing energy intensity of hydro transport up to 15% and operating costs.
  • * Less vibration resulting from delivery pumps, compensation of heat, linear and temperature expansion.
